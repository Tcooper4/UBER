Pricing Parameters

Rider price sensitivity (ε) -0.70
More negative = riders more price-sensitive. Cohen 2016: ε ≈ −0.5 to −1.0 during surge.

How aggressively Lyft steals riders when Uber surges. 1.0 = use observed data; 1.5 = Lyft 50% more aggressive.
Max allowable surge (price ceiling) 200%
Real Uber surge caps around 3-5x. Lower this to see effect of regulatory caps.

Supply Parameters

Fraction of demand spike that drivers can serve via pre-positioning. Hall 2015: 30-70% range.
